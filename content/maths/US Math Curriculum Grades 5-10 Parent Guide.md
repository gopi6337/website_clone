# US Math Curriculum for Grades 5-10
## Parent Information Guide

### Overview
This guide provides a clear overview of what your child will learn in US mathematics education from grades 5 through 10. Our curriculum aligns with Common Core State Standards and prepares students for advanced mathematics, college, and career success.

---

## Grade 5: Building Strong Foundations

**Key Topics:**
- **Fractions**: Addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division of fractions
- **Decimals**: Operations with decimals and understanding place value
- **Whole Numbers**: Multi-digit multiplication and division
- **Volume**: Understanding and calculating volume of 3D shapes
- **Coordinate Plane**: Graphing points and solving problems

**Why It Matters:** Grade 5 establishes critical number sense that builds the foundation for algebra.

---

## Grade 6: Introduction to Algebra

**Key Topics:**
- **Ratios & Proportions**: Understanding relationships between quantities
- **Negative Numbers**: Introduction to the complete number system
- **Fractions**: Dividing fractions and working with rational numbers
- **Basic Algebra**: Writing and solving simple equations
- **Statistics**: Understanding data, graphs, and distributions
- **Geometry**: Area, surface area, and volume of shapes

**Why It Matters:** Grade 6 bridges arithmetic and algebra, introducing algebraic thinking.

---

## Grade 7: Proportional Thinking & Linear Equations

**Key Topics:**
- **Percentages**: Real-world applications including discounts and interest
- **Rational Numbers**: All operations with positive and negative numbers
- **Linear Equations**: Solving multi-step equations and inequalities
- **Geometry**: Scale drawings, angles, area, and volume of circles and prisms
- **Probability**: Understanding chance and making predictions
- **Statistics**: Analyzing data from samples and populations

**Why It Matters:** Grade 7 deepens algebraic reasoning and prepares students for high school math.

---

## Grade 8: Algebra Fundamentals

**Key Topics:**
- **Linear Equations**: Solving systems of equations and graphing lines
- **Functions**: Understanding function concepts and relationships
- **Exponents**: Working with powers and scientific notation
- **Pythagorean Theorem**: Right triangle calculations
- **Geometry**: Transformations, congruence, and similarity
- **Irrational Numbers**: Understanding √2, π, and other non-repeating decimals
- **Volume**: Cylinders, cones, and spheres

**Why It Matters:** Grade 8 completes middle school foundations and prepares students for Algebra I and Geometry.

---

## Grade 9: Algebra I (High School)

**Key Topics:**
- **Linear Functions**: Graphing, writing, and analyzing linear relationships
- **Systems of Equations**: Solving multiple equations simultaneously
- **Quadratic Equations**: Factoring, solving, and graphing parabolas
- **Exponential Functions**: Growth and decay in real-world contexts
- **Polynomials**: Adding, subtracting, multiplying, and factoring
- **Radical Expressions**: Simplifying and solving square root equations
- **Data Analysis**: Statistics, probability, and data interpretation

**Why It Matters:** Algebra I is essential for college readiness and develops logical reasoning skills applicable to all STEM fields.

---

## Grade 10: Geometry

**Key Topics:**
- **Geometric Reasoning**: Writing proofs and logical arguments
- **Triangles**: Congruence, similarity, and special right triangles
- **Trigonometry**: Sine, cosine, tangent, and their applications
- **Circles**: Properties, equations, arcs, and sectors
- **Coordinate Geometry**: Distance, midpoint, and slope formulas
- **Transformations**: Translations, rotations, reflections, and dilations
- **3D Geometry**: Surface area and volume of complex solids
- **Introduction to Trigonometric Functions**: Understanding angles and ratios

**Why It Matters:** Geometry develops spatial reasoning and proof-writing skills essential for higher mathematics, engineering, and architecture.
